# LUMENFALL — Arcaea-Inspired Research

## Arcaea mechanics to mirror

- Dual-plane input: floor taps/holds plus sky lane arcs and holds
- Strict timing judgement ladder with score and skill systems
- Chart complexity via simultaneous streams, flicks, and arc traces
- High-contrast futuristic UI with motion readability
- Song unlock progression and tiered difficulties
- Story fragments increase retention between sessions

## Addictive loop patterns

- Hit -> glow -> combo -> audio accent feedback loop
- Near-miss recovery with tighter windows at high difficulty
- Daily mission and streak bonuses
- Fever/overdrive visual transformation
- Sub-2-second restart to next attempt
- Personal best and grade shown before replay
- Short first chart with climax at ~70% timeline
