# PINTEREST — design culture & visual mood study

> Not a fashion brand — a visual research platform. Pinterest is where every brand on this list builds their moodboards. Study Pinterest the way you study a museum: what's saved, what's reshared, what reaches "trending" tells you what the visual culture is about to do.

## Why Pinterest matters for FTC
- It's the closest thing the design world has to a global brain.
- Pinterest's trending boards are a 3-6 month leading indicator for what Instagram (and then runway) does.
- Customers literally moodboard their wardrobe before buying. FTC concepts must be Pinterest-saveable.

## What FTC concepts must do to perform on Pinterest
1. **Saveable at thumbnail.** The image must read at 200×300px. Test every concept by squinting at a 1-inch preview.
2. **Single subject, generous negative space.** Pinterest's vertical 2:3 format rewards composition with breathing room.
3. **Tonal, not high-contrast.** High-contrast images burn out at thumbnail size. Tonal images keep readability.
4. **No text on image.** Pinterest's algorithm down-ranks heavy text. FTC's restraint serves us here.

## SEO on Pinterest
- Pin descriptions count. Use natural language: "Luxury Christian streetwear hoodie in bone, heavyweight 320gsm garment-washed cotton, tonal embroidery."
- Hashtags work but sparingly (3–5). #luxurystreetwear #christianstreetwear #quietluxury #lemaire #editorial
- Repins are the metric. Boards over 1k followers feed the algorithm.

## Pinterest's design language — what to lift
1. **Vertical 2:3 ratio.** Build FTC catalog imagery in this ratio first; everything else (1:1, 16:9) derives from it.
2. **Saturation discipline.** Pinterest's best-performing images sit in the 30-55% saturation band. FTC's 0.55 cap is perfectly aligned.
3. **Cohesive board aesthetic.** A FTC board should feel like one image scrolled vertically — same lighting, same palette, same tonal mood across every pin.

## Algorithmic insight
- Pinterest "Idea Pins" (video carousel) push organically harder than static pins as of 2024–2025. FTC should produce 5–10 Idea Pins per drop showing: fabric close-up → silhouette walk → final fit pic.
- Pinterest "Shop" tab integrates with Shopify directly. FTC should sync product catalog so saved pins become shoppable.

## Failure modes
- **Too much text in pins.** Algorithm punishes it.
- **High saturation.** Reads cheap at thumbnail.
- **Inconsistent ratios.** Mixed 1:1, 16:9, 2:3 in one board fragments the aesthetic.
- **Generic stock imagery.** Pinterest punishes stock; rewards original photography.

## TL;DR — apply to FTC
1. **Build FTC concept imagery 2:3 vertical first.**
2. **Tonal, low-contrast, single-subject composition.**
3. **Build 6 FTC Pinterest boards** (Cornerstone Drop, Materials, Moodboard, Editorial, Architecture, Quiet Faith).
4. **Track Pinterest Trends Tool weekly** for "christian streetwear" and "quiet luxury" search velocity.
